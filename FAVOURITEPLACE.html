FAVOURITE PLACE

Ha Long bay

Among all the beautiful destinations in Vietnam, my favorite place is Ha Long Bay. Every time I think about it, a wave of excitement flows through me as if I were already standing on the deck of a yacht gazing at its breathtaking scenery. Ha Long Bay is famous for its emerald-green waters and thousands of limestone islands rising dramatically from the sea. Each island has its own unique shapeâ€”some look like giant dragons, others like turtles, and some even resemble human figures. The surface of the water glitters under the sunshine, and when the mist falls, the bay suddenly turns into a mysterious painting. The seagulls flying above add a touch of liveliness to this natural wonder. One of the most unforgettable experiences is taking a yacht trip across the bay. Standing on the deck, I can feel the fresh breeze brushing against my face while watching the golden sunset slowly melt into the horizon. At night, the whole bay sparkles with lights from other yachts, creating a dreamlike atmosphere. Besides the scenery, Ha Long Bay is also a paradise of delicious seafood. From freshly grilled squid and steamed crabs to sweet clams and oysters, every dish carries the pure taste of the ocean. Sitting on the yacht, enjoying a seafood feast while listening to the soft sound of waves, is truly an unforgettable memory. For me, Ha Long Bay is not just a tourist attractionâ€”it is a magical place that always fills me with joy and inspiration.

My plan when travelling Ha Long bay

Day Plan
Day 1 Board a luxury yacht, sail through limestone islands, relax on the sundeck, and enjoy a seafood dinner.
Day 2 Explore hidden caves and lagoons, go kayaking, visit a floating village, and enjoy dinner under the stars.
Day 3 Hike on Ti Top Island, swim and sunbathe, relax with spa services, and join a barbecue party on deck.
Day 4 Watch the sunrise on the yacht, return to the harbor, enjoy a local restaurant, then travel back to hometown.
