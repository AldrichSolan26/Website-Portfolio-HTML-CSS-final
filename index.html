Fun fact about my self

One of the most interesting and somewhat funny facts about me is that I once had a three-year relationship in one of the strictest schools imaginable. This was not just any regular school — it was a highly disciplined boarding school where students were not allowed to use mobile phones, and romantic relationships were completely forbidden. If the school found out that a student was in a relationship, the punishment could be as severe as expulsion. On top of that, the workload and academic pressure were intense, with constant monitoring and regular evaluations. Despite all these restrictions, I started dating someone in grade 10, and we managed to keep the relationship going all the way until the end of grade 12. It was not easy at all. We had to be very creative to communicate and spend time together without getting caught. Small gestures like exchanging handwritten notes or finding a quiet corner during break time meant a lot. It was like living in our own secret little world inside a strict and controlled environment. After graduating, we were finally free from the school rules, and we thought our relationship would only get stronger. Ironically, just four months after leaving school, we broke up. Looking back, I find it both amusing and memorable — we survived three years of strict rules and secrecy, only to part ways when we had complete freedom. It taught me that sometimes the challenge is what makes a story exciting, and even though it ended, it’s still a chapter of my life that makes people smile when they hear it.
